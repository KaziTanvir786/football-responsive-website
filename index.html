Copa America 2021

Argentina won its first major title in 28 years on Sunday when an Angel Di Maria goal gave the side a 1-0 win over Brazil and a record-equalling 15th Copa America trophy. Di Maria started for just the second time in the Copa. Renan Lodi failed to cut out a long ball forward from Rodrigo De Paul and Di Maria lobbed the stranded Ederson with aplomb.
